Ways you can you help the Environment

These are some ways you can help protect the environment at home. It’s just a matter of making a commitment to doing something good for the planet and sticking to that plan.

  1. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
  2. Compost
  3. Sustainable food products – packaging
  4. Supporting eco – friendly businesses
  5. Get involved - Donate to Eco – Friendly organisations (your time or money)
  6. Stay informed by reading reputable sources
  7. Electricity – Turn off the lights and devices when you don’t need them, heating
  8. Only wash clothes when you have a full load
  9. Reduce your carbon footprint - Try take public transport if you can, energy efficient car or cycle
  10. Plant trees and pollinator friendly plants in your garden, compost (organic no pesticides) bee kind
  11. Shop locally & organically
  12. Inspire others
  13. Use reusable products
  14. Pick up litter – organise a clean up day in your town
